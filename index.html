InternLink has a robust matching process that pairs learners in corporate-sponsored learnerships with SMEs based on their skills, interests, and the

Participating learners gain real-world work experience, which complements their learnerships and enhances their professional portfolios. This experience improves their chances of finding employment after completing the program

InternLink acts as the 'Employer of Record,' handling administrative tasks and employing the learners on behalf of host companies. This arrangement reduces the administrative burden for companies, allowing them to focus on mentoring and training the interns.

While not mandatory, SMEs have the option to top up the stipend provided to interns if they wish to do so. However, the core benefit for SMEs lies in accessing motivated interns who can contribute to their workforce over a 12-month period without any financial risk.
